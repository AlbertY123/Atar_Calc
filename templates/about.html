About

This tool predicts an approximate ATAR from predicted raw VCE study scores. It uses public VTAC PDFs:

  • VTAC Scaling Report (2024)
  • VTAC Aggregate → ATAR table (2024)

How it works

  1. Approximate scaling by interpolating the published rounded points (raw 20/25/…/50).
  2. Compute scaled aggregate: top 4 + 10% of 5th and 6th.
  3. Convert aggregate to ATAR via interpolation of the official table.
Important: this is not official advice or an official calculator. Your actual ATAR depends on cohort performance, subject combinations, and VTAC’s full scaling process.
